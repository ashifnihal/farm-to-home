About Farm to Home

A family legacy of three brothers bridging the gap between India's finest mango orchards and your doorstep through technology and tradition

Our Story

Born and raised in the mango heartland of Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh, the Nihal brothers grew up among sprawling orchards that have been in their family for three generations. The sweet aroma of ripening mangoes, the sight of golden fruits hanging from ancient trees, and the taste of sun-kissed Alphonso mangoes were integral parts of their childhood.

After pursuing engineering degrees from JNTUA (Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur), both brothers embarked on successful careers in technology. Ashif became an AI & ML Engineer at a Big4 company, while Arif built his career as an IT professional in Singapore, specializing in operations and supply chain management. Despite their success in the corporate world, they never forgot their roots in the mango orchards of Chittoor.

During their visits home, they witnessed a growing disconnect between urban consumers and authentic farm-fresh produce. City dwellers were paying premium prices for mangoes that had traveled thousands of kilometers, losing their natural sweetness and aroma in cold storage. Meanwhile, their family's orchards produced some of the finest mangoes in India, but lacked the reach to connect directly with consumers who would truly appreciate their quality.

In 2020, inspired by their agricultural heritage and armed with their technical expertise, the Nihal brothers founded Farm to Home. Their vision was simple yet revolutionary: leverage cutting-edge technology to bring the unparalleled taste of tree-ripened Indian mangoes directly to doorsteps across the country, while supporting sustainable farming practices and empowering local farmers.

Ashif's expertise in AI and machine learning enabled the development of precision farming solutions that optimize yield and quality. His algorithms analyze soil conditions, weather patterns, and tree health to ensure each mango reaches peak ripeness. Arif's supply chain expertise ensures that mangoes harvested at dawn reach customers' doorsteps within 48 hours, preserving their natural sweetness and aroma.

Today, Farm to Home stands as a testament to the perfect blend of tradition and technology. We're not just delivering mangoes; we're sharing a piece of our heritage, our passion, and our commitment to quality with every box we send. Each mango carries the legacy of three generations of farming expertise, enhanced by modern technology, and delivered with the care and attention it deserves.

Co-Founder & Chief Technology Officer

Education: Bachelor's in Computer Science, JNTUA
Expertise: AI & Machine Learning, Precision Farming, Agricultural Technology

Ashif combines his deep technical expertise with his family's agricultural heritage to revolutionize mango farming. His AI-driven precision farming solutions analyze thousands of data points daily—from soil moisture levels to weather patterns—ensuring each tree receives optimal care. Beyond technology, Ashif is committed to empowering local farmers with modern tools and knowledge, bridging the gap between traditional farming wisdom and cutting-edge agricultural technology.

Arif Nihal

Arif brings a global perspective and extensive experience in operations and supply chain management to Farm to Home.
